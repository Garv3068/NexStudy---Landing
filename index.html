Why is it free?

Most apps charge you because they pay for the AI compute. NexStudy connects directly to Google's Gemini API using your own free key.

  • Unlimited Usage Solve 10 or 1,000 questions. No limits.
  • Privacy First No login required. We don't store your data. 
# The Magic behind NexStudy
import google.generativeai as genai

def solve_doubt(image, user_key):
    genai.configure(api_key=user_key)
    model = genai.GenerativeModel('gemini-2.0-flash')
    return model.generate_content([image, "Solve this"])
